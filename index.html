Why are they the GOAT?

Steve Wozniak is a genius when it comes to computers. He basically single handily created the Apple I. Apple would not be where it is today without him.

Apple I

Wozniak created the very first Apple computer, basically on his own. This was the beginning of Apple's major growth as a company.

Biography

Stephen Gary Wozniak was born on August 11, 1950 in San Jose, California. He is currently married to Janet Hill. His children are Jesse John Wozniak, Sara Nadine Wozniak, and Stephen Gary Wozniak Jr. He is the co-founder of Apple Inc.
